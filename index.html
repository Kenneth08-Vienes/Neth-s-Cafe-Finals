What kind of Coffee we serve for you

BASED COFFEE

Cappuccino

₱85

Ingredients: Espresso, steamed milk, milk foam.

Description: Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam, typically layered with the foam on top.

Americano

₱75

Ingredients: Espresso, hot water.

Description: Made by diluting espresso with hot water, resulting in a coffee with a similar strength to drip coffee but with a richer flavor.

Espresso

₱50

Ingredients: Finely ground coffee beans, hot water.

Description: A small, concentrated coffee shot with a strong flavor.

Macchiato

₱65

Ingredients: Espresso, small amount of milk foam.

Description: Espresso “marked” with a small amount of milk foam, giving it a slightly creamier texture without much added milk.

Mocha

₱90

Ingredients: Espresso, chocolate syrup or cocoa powder, steamed milk, whipped cream (optional).

Description: A chocolate-flavored latte, often topped with whipped cream for added sweetness.

Coffee Latte

₱55

Ingredients: Espresso, steamed milk, light milk foam.

Description: Similar to a cappuccino but with more steamed milk and a thinner layer of foam.

Flat White

₱100

Ingredients: Espresso, steamed milk (microfoam).

Description: Made with a higher ratio of espresso to milk, with microfoam giving it a creamy texture without excess foam.

COLD COFFEE

Iced Coffee

₱85

Ingredients: Brewed coffee, ice, optional milk or sweetener.

Description: Regular brewed coffee that’s cooled and poured over ice. Milk or sweeteners can be added to taste.

Iced Latte

₱75

Ingredients: Espresso, cold milk, ice.

Description: A chilled latte with espresso and cold milk poured over ice. It has a creamier texture than regular iced coffee due to the milk.

Cold Brew

₱50

Ingredients: Coarse-ground coffee, cold water, ice.

Description: Made by steeping coffee grounds in cold water for 12-24 hours, then straining. It’s smoother and less acidic than iced coffee due to the slow brewing process.

Iced Mocha

₱65

Ingredients: Espresso or strong coffee, chocolate syrup, cold milk, ice, optional whipped cream.

Description: A chocolate-flavored iced latte with espresso, chocolate syrup, and cold milk. It’s often topped with whipped cream for added sweetness.

SPECIALTY COFFEE

Affogato

₱85

Ingredients: Espresso, vanilla ice cream or gelato.

Description: A dessert-like coffee drink where a shot of hot espresso is poured over a scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato, creating a delicious contrast between hot and cold, creamy and strong flavors.

Irish Coffee

₱75

Ingredients: Hot coffee, Irish whiskey, brown sugar, whipped cream.

Description: A warm, alcoholic coffee drink made by mixing hot coffee with Irish whiskey and brown sugar, then topping it with a layer of thick whipped cream.

Irish Coffee

₱50

Ingredients: Very finely ground coffee, water, sugar (optional), and sometimes cardamom.

Description: Traditionally prepared by boiling very finely ground coffee with water (and sugar if desired) in a special pot called a cezve or ibrik. It’s served unfiltered, so the grounds settle at the bottom, and sometimes has a hint of cardamom for extra flavor.
